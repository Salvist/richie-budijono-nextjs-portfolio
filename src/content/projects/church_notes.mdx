## Context

Church Notes began with a small product observation: people taking notes during a sermon or study session frequently interrupt their writing to search for a passage. I designed and built a mobile experience that keeps that context inside the note itself.

## The product

The editor recognizes scripture references and brings relevant verses from multiple translations into the writing flow. Notes remain local and fast, while a familiar rich-text editor gives people enough structure without making the app feel heavy.

## Technical decisions

- **Flutter** for one cohesive mobile interface.
- **SQLite** for private, fast, offline-first note storage.
- **Quill** for a flexible rich-text editing foundation.
